## Detailed Comparison

### eDoc Viewer (This Site)

**Best for**: Quick viewing, privacy-conscious users, mobile access

#### Advantages
- ✅ **No Installation** - Works immediately in any modern browser
- ✅ **Complete Privacy** - Files never leave your device
- ✅ **Cross-Platform** - Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android
- ✅ **Offline PWA** - Install and use without internet
- ✅ **Free Forever** - Open-source, no premium tiers
- ✅ **Document Preview** - View PDFs, Office docs, images inline
- ✅ **Mobile-Friendly** - Responsive design for tablets and phones

#### Limitations
- ⚠️ **Browser Dependency** - Requires modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- ⚠️ **Memory Limits** - Very large files (>500MB) may have issues
- ⚠️ **No Signing** - View-only, cannot create signed documents

#### Best Use Cases
- Opening eDoc files on the go
- Quick verification on any device
- Privacy-sensitive documents
- Users without admin rights to install software

---

### DigiDoc4 (Desktop Application)

**Best for**: Regular users, document signing, power users

#### Advantages
- ✅ **Full Signing Capability** - Create and sign new eDoc files
- ✅ **eID Card Support** - Direct integration with national ID cards
- ✅ **No File Size Limits** - Handle very large files
- ✅ **Official Application** - Maintained by RIA (Estonia)
- ✅ **Advanced Features** - Batch operations, encryption
- ✅ **Offline** - No internet required

#### Limitations
- ⚠️ **Installation Required** - Needs admin rights
- ⚠️ **Platform-Specific** - Separate downloads for each OS
- ⚠️ **Update Management** - Must keep updated manually
- ⚠️ **Complexity** - More features mean steeper learning curve

#### Best Use Cases
- Creating and signing eDoc files
- Using national eID cards
- Regular professional use
- Handling very large files

---

### Adobe Acrobat Reader/Pro

**Best for**: PDF-centric workflows

#### Advantages
- ✅ **Industry Standard** - Widely used and trusted
- ✅ **PDF Focus** - Excellent PDF viewing and editing
- ✅ **Signing** - Can create digital signatures (Pro version)
- ✅ **Integration** - Works with many business systems

#### Limitations
- ❌ **ASiC-E Support** - Limited or no native support for .edoc/.asice
- ⚠️ **Cost** - Pro version required for full features
- ⚠️ **Container Handling** - Must extract files manually from ASiC-E
- ⚠️ **Signature Verification** - May not recognize EU certificate authorities

#### Best Use Cases
- PDF-only workflows
- Users who already have Adobe Pro
- When eDoc files contain only PDFs

---

### Government Portals (Various)

**Best for**: Official verification, legal processes

Examples: eParaksts.lv (Latvia), id.ee (Estonia)

#### Advantages
- ✅ **Official Verification** - Government-trusted validation
- ✅ **Integration** - Direct connection to national services
- ✅ **Legal Standing** - Officially recognized verification
- ✅ **No Installation** - Web-based access

#### Limitations
- ❌ **Internet Required** - Cannot work offline
- ⚠️ **Country-Specific** - Often limited to national documents
- ⚠️ **File Size Limits** - Often 10-50MB maximum
- ⚠️ **Privacy** - Files uploaded to government servers
- ⚠️ **Availability** - Dependent on portal uptime

#### Best Use Cases
- Official legal verification
- Government document submission
- When official validation record is needed

---

## Privacy Comparison

### 🔒 Most Private: eDoc Viewer & DigiDoc4
- **Processing**: 100% local on your device
- **Data Transmission**: Zero - files never leave your computer
- **Logging**: None
- **Best for**: Sensitive documents, personal data, confidential business files

### ⚠️ Moderate Privacy: Adobe Acrobat
- **Processing**: Local by default, optional cloud features
- **Data Transmission**: Minimal (license verification)
- **Logging**: Adobe telemetry (can be disabled)
- **Best for**: Standard business documents

### 🌐 Server-Side: Government Portals
- **Processing**: Files uploaded to servers
- **Data Transmission**: Full file upload required
- **Logging**: Yes, for audit purposes
- **Best for**: Official submissions where server processing is expected

All viewers listed here are safe when obtained from official sources:

- **DigiDoc4** - Official government software
- **Adobe Acrobat** - Established commercial software
- **Government Portals** - Official state services

### Security Tips
